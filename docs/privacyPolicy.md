# Privacy Policy for Neighborhood Noise

**Effective Date:** December 11, 2025

## Introduction

Neighborhood Noise ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application for discovering local bands and live music.

## Information We Collect

**Camera Access:** Our app requires access to your device camera to capture photos and videos of bands, performances, and music-related content. We only access your camera when you actively use this feature within the app.

**Photos and Videos:** Any photos or videos you capture through the app are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them with the community.

**Location Data:** We collect approximate location information to help you discover bands and live music events in your area.

**Account Information:** If you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address.

## How We Use Your Information

- To help you discover local bands and live music events
- To enable sharing of band-related photos and videos with other users
- To connect you with the local music community
- To improve our services and user experience

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your data when:
- You explicitly choose to share content with other users
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your account and associated data at any time
- Revoke camera permissions through your device settings
- Request a copy of your data

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
